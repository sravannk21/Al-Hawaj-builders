02

Construction Supervision

Construction Supervision entails a continuous and consistent scrutiny and inspection of the building in progress. These inspections ensure that every work takes place at the right time and in the right way. Construction Supervisors also find out the shortcomings in the building and take measures to rectify them. Construction supervisors also make sure that the construction is taking place in accordance with the plan and other contract documents.
