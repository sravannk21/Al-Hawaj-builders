our organizational spirit belives that where design meets passion & love with space, good architecture happens here

Urban Design

Framing the sketch of towns, cities and broader landscapes is termed as Urban Design. Urban Design also includes designing residential areas, grassy lawns and industrial estates. Al-Hawaj embarks upon this grand endeavour to work out procedures and redefine architecture that benefits both the constructors and the residents. Proper analysis of the suburbs is inevitable to create an apt urban design. We formulate precise and brilliant strategies to come up with designs that fit the purpose of the construction.

Landscape Design

Landscape Design requires ingenious creativity since it demands an alteration in the natural being of a landscape. The gardens and the lawns are modified to suit the fantasies of the owners. This decorative modification is done with utmost care not to tamper with the organic harmony of the nature. Al-Hawaj conducts precise site analysis and research to create meticulously planned strategies to craft beautiful residential environments. We prove ourselves to be the masters in restructuring open and closed places alike.

Blueprint Design

Blueprint is a detailed sketch of the elements necessary to finish the construction. Blueprints are mostly two-dimensional images. Blueprints represent the project plan and the basic idea of the building. Various contractors and architects frame blueprints at various stages of construction. Blueprints are usually designed at the preconstruction phase. Later, it can also be modified as per the requirements. Blueprints consist of site plans, floor plans, planning of elevators and the plumbing details.

Industrial Design

Industrial Design which is also called Design-build construction amalgamates various dimensions of constructing the building. In order to create an industrial design we collaborate with various engineers and architects to create a single whole entity. Industrial Design makes sure that every department of the construction works in harmony with one another. Industrial Design helps the constructors to combine, collaborate, and coordinate efficiently
